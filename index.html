What does Calorie Calculator do?

A common problem that people trying to lose or maintain weight encounter is the fact that they might not be considering how large caloric intake plays a part in it. Exercise and good nutrition do play a part in weight management, but skipping out on or glossing over calorie intake will be a problem. Although many people do count their calories, it can be difficult to calculate the amount they lose from exercising. A problem with counting calories is that not all foods will have a nutrition label, and googling it every time is a hassle. Another problem that stacks on top of that is keeping track of consumed and expended calories in one place. Calorie Calculator makes calorie tracking efficient and easy, with calorie tracking all in one place.
